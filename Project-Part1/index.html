Introduction & A Brief history

Welcome to Freeze Flame Productions Inc.!

Since our establishment in 1995, we've evolved from a Recording Artist Management Company into a multifaceted organization encompassing a Record Label, Media Production Company, Financial Services, and Publishing. Our journey began as Red Brown Entertainment in 2003, and with the support of an emerging artist, we incorporated in 2006.

Today, Freeze Flame Productions Inc. thrives on diverse projects and revenue streams, operating under specific names tailored to our services. We offer a quality facility for our talent roster and clientele to hone their craft at competitive rates.

As a registered company, we are proud to be a Registered Music Publisher with SOCAN (Canada) and ASCAP (USA), participating in various royalty programs for mechanicals, videos, streaming, and more.
